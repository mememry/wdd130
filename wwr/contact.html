Rapidz Whitewater Rafting

Rapids Whitewater Rafting is located in White Salmon, Washington, right on the line of the Cascades. With access to the best rivers in the Pacific Northwest you can enjoy tranquil views, thrilling Class V rapids, and everything in between. Let us take you on the adventure of a lifetime.

Meet our staff!

Katy likes to get back to basics when she's not in the office. Although she has taken many rafting trips, she also likes to go hiking on her days off.

John can't stay away from the water. When he wants to relax, he takes a kayak out and "just paddles around."

Suz has been rafting since she was a child. Her parents were denizens of the river and took her with them. She took her first Class V trip when she was only thirteen.

Mark and Pete are dedicated rafters. When they aren't guiding beginner excursions, they often go on rafting trips just for fun. They say it's nice to relax and enjoy the ride.
